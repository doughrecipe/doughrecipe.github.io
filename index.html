Instructions:

  1. Mix Cold and Hot Water.
  2. Mix Yeast and Salt into Water Mixture.
  3. Mix Water Mixture and Yeast.
  4. Stir until ball forms.
  5. Knead dough for 10 minutes.
  6. Let rise in warm place for 90-120 minutes.
  7. Divide dough into balls and place into separate bowls covered in plastic wrap.
  8. Let rise for another 20 minutes.
